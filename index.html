The Studio

Run by a lifter,
not a franchise.

Hunk Fitness Studio was built around one idea: training should be led by someone who has actually competed, not just certified. The studio's owner is a competitive bodybuilder, and that discipline runs through every program, every rack, and every rep count on the floor.

Members consistently point to the same things: a floor full of modern, well‑maintained equipment, trainers who correct form instead of just counting sets, and a space that stays motivating even during the 6:30 AM shift.

  • Owner is a competitive bodybuilder, training members with contest‑level attention to form
  • Modern strength & bodybuilding equipment on a spacious, accessible main‑road floor
  • Hands‑on trainers — members frequently praise the coaching, not just the machines
  • Located directly on Kona Expressway, near the Carry Road petrol pump, Shibpur
