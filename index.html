The Studio

Run by a lifter,
built for everyone.

Hunk Fitness Studio was built around one idea: training should be led by someone who has actually competed, not just certified. The studio's owner is a competitive bodybuilder, and that discipline runs through every program on the floor.

It's a genuinely co-ed studio — certified male and female trainers work side by side, so everyone trains with someone they're comfortable with. Add in diet consultation and personalized workout plans, and it's built to be the only fitness stop you need.

  • Owner is a competitive bodybuilder, coaching with contest-level attention to form
  • Certified male & female trainers — a genuinely co-ed training floor
  • Personalized diet consultation alongside every training program
  • Modern strength & bodybuilding equipment on a spacious, main-road floor
  • Located directly on Kona Expressway, near the Carry Road petrol pump, Shibpur
